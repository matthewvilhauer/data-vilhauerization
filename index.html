World Cup Analysis

My first visualization gives a visual comparison of a number of different statistics for soccer teams in the 2010 world cup. Click on the buttons to see a visual comparison of each stat. Running the mouse over a give nteam's circle populates the modal with that team's data.

Designed by Matt Vilhauer, inspired by Mike Bostock and d3
